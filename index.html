Welcome to ASILS13

"Asian Symposium Intense Laser (ASILS-13): Unraveling the Frontiers of Laser-Driven Phenomena"
Laser Plasma Research Facilities in India: TIFR Mumbai | IIT Hyderabad | TIFR Hyderabad.

Objectives of ASILS13

The aim of this symposium is to introduce undergraduate/graduate students to the exciting field of ultra-short,ultra-intense Laser-Matter interaction.

Topics of the Symposium

  • High to Ultra-high Laser Intensity Science
  • Attosecond Science and High Harmonic Generation
  • Laser-Driven Particle Acceleration
  • Ultrafast Plasma Dynamics
  • Computational Plasma Physics
